## About

Ezra Dombowsky (known online as Ezra Dom / ezradom) is a freelance brand strategist and designer based in Kelowna, British Columbia, Canada. He is the founder of Luminary Graphix — a strategy-first brand identity studio serving small businesses, founders, and entrepreneurs in the Okanagan and beyond.

Ezra specialises in building brands that compete on identity rather than price: archetype-driven strategy, brand positioning, and visual identity design. He has built 10+ brands and completed 20+ logo and design projects. He has been accepted to IED Milano's Masters in Brand Strategy & Management program (beginning November 2026).

Primary keywords: brand strategist BC Canada, freelance brand designer Kelowna, brand strategy for small business, brand identity designer Okanagan.
